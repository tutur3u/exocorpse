About Exocorpse

Exocorpse is where I put the intense parts of my character work: loyalty, survival, devotion, performance, body language, soft horror, and people trying to become something sharper than their pain.

Fenrys lens

sharp light, loud posture, movement before mercy

I love attacks that make a character look like they have just stepped out of a fight, a stage, or a bad decision they fully believe in.

Morris lens

quiet dread, written symbols, softness with a hidden knife

I love psychological details: mirrored expressions, coded notes, memory motifs, and the moment before someone says the honest thing.

How I play

surprise me with a scene, a symbol, or a small story

I bookmark characters that spark a visual idea, mix revenges with friendly attacks when I can, and adore thoughtful interpretation.

Design Notes

Little things that make attacks feel personal

  • Lean into asymmetry, expressive hands, and posture with intent.
  • Pulse can be sharp, theatrical, forward, and physically loud.
  • Neuro can be quiet, symbolic, coded, intimate, and unsettling.
  • Outfit changes, AU details, and prop symbolism are very welcome.
  • Written captions, UI labels, and fake notes are loved.
