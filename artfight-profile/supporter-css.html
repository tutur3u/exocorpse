About Exocorpse

I love illustrative pieces, story and dialogue writing, sprite work, expressive characters, and atmospheric worldbuilding. Exocorpse mixes anatomy, psychology, terminal interfaces, and dramatic character storytelling into one dark creative space.

Prototype: Pulse

Action, combat, confident poses, dramatic lighting, body language, movement, teeth, bloodless body horror, weapons, and tactical outfits.

Great for bold visual attacks

Prototype: Neuro

Eerie portraits, symbolism, written details, character relationships, memory motifs, coded imagery, soft horror, and narrative-driven scenes.

Great for story-heavy attacks
